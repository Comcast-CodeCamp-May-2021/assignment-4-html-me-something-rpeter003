DO NOT FEED WILDLIFE!!!


Animals

While up in Florissant, Co staying in a cabin. We got to see a few animals. While we were inside relaxing in the early evening the bear walked past just roaming the area. The deer walked up to the door looking to have us give them food. As a responsible person we did not feed the deer as that is the appropriate thing to do.






Views

The views were amazing!! we could see Pikes Peak and the mountains were all around. In my opinion the views were the best part of the vacation!




Weather

While we were there the weather stayed cloudy and windy but stil comfortable. The average tempatures up there the end of May stayed in the low 60's. We did have breaks of sun which just made it all more beautiful. The last night we were up there it rained and there was a little thunder and lightning but did not ruin the trip at all.
